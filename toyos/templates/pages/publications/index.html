Publications

2024

During my internship at the French Alternative Energies and Atomic Energy Commission (CEA), I designed and developed a decentralized notarization system for the Ethereum platform. This work was presented as a live demo at the BRAINS conference:

  • G. Toyos-Marfurt, R. Abdallah and Á. García-Pérez. "Notara: Eﬃcient Blockchain Asset Notarization Service", 2024 6th Conference on Blockchain Research & Applications for Innovative Networks and Services (BRAINS), Berlin, Germany, 2024, pp. 1-3.

My master research project at the Laboratoire Traitement et Communication de l'Information (LTCI) of Institut Polytechnique de Paris has been accepted for publication at SIROCCO:

  • G. Toyos-Marfurt and P. Kuznetsov, "On the Bit Complexity of Iterated Memory", 2024, to be published at 31st International Colloquium on Structural Information and Communication Complexity (SIROCCO), Vietri sul Mare, Italy, 2024.
