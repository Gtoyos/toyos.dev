Notes

These are notes from courses and books that you might find useful. I often take notes in Markdown while studying course materials or write down key insights shared by my professors during lectures. After purchasing a tablet with a stylus, I transitioned to handwritten notes, as they offer a more versatile and creative format. The images included in these notes are sourced from the original materials I used for studying. All copyrights and credits belong to their respective authors.

Most notes are in Spanish. I believe translating concepts or ideas from one language to another is an excellent exercise. Constructing sentences that convey the same meaning in different languages helps you explore concepts from multiple perspectives and assess your understanding more deeply.
