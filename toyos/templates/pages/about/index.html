About Toyos.dev

This website serves two purposes. Firstly, it is a platform for showcasing my professional endeavors and interests, allowing people to learn more about my work. You just have to visit toyos.dev, where you can take a look at my projects with a few clicks. Moreover, it allows me to present myself in a more personal way than a resume would.

Secondly, this site is a little counter-movement against the current internet trend of centralization. Where most web traffic is handled by a handful of domains. Maintaining a personal website can be seen as having a small bastion of freedom for self-expression. I hope to encourage fellow internet users to explore the broader possibilities of the World Wide Web, it's up to us to make it an intresting place.
